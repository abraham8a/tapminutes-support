TapMinutes turns your meeting recordings into structured minutes. We've designed it to collect as little personal information as possible. This policy explains what we collect, why, and how it's handled.

01

Who we are

02

What we collect

When you generate minutes for a recording, the following data leaves your device and is processed by our servers:

Audio recordings
Your meeting audio is sent to our server for transcription.
Transcripts & prompts
Transcribed meeting text and any custom style instructions you type are sent to generate your minutes.
Device identifier
A random ID generated on first launch. Used only to enforce free-tier limits and prevent abuse. Not linked to your name, email, Apple ID, or any other identifying information.

We do not collect

Name Email address Contacts Location Photos Health data Financial info Ad identifiers Analytics
03

How we use your data

The audio and transcripts you send us are used only to generate the meeting minutes you requested.

We do not use your recordings or transcripts to train AI models, sell them, share them with advertisers, or analyze them for any other purpose.
04

Subprocessors

To generate transcripts and minutes, requests are routed through a Cloudflare Worker which forwards them to OpenAI's API. OpenAI processes the audio and text and returns results to us.

Your device
→
Cloudflare Worker
→
OpenAI API
→
Your minutes

Per OpenAI's API policy, content submitted via the API is not used to train their models.

05

Live captions

On-device only

The live captions shown on screen while you record are generated entirely on your device using Apple's on-device Speech framework. That text is never sent to our servers.

06

On-device storage & iCloud

Your recordings, transcripts, and generated minutes are stored on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, they may also sync to your personal iCloud account.

We do not have access to your iCloud data — that relationship is between you and Apple.

07

Retention

Audio files and transcripts are processed in real time and are not retained on our servers after the response is returned. Logs may contain anonymized usage counts (e.g., minutes used per device per day) for rate limiting, retained for up to 30 days.

08
